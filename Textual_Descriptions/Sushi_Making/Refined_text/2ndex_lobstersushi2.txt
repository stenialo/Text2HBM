If frozen, thaw the Langoustine tails.
Squeeze out the excess water. 
Loosely chop the Langoustine tails into cubes.
Place into a small mixing bowl.
Cut the cucumber in half.
Take the one half and cut it long ways. 
Slice the cucumber to half.
Peel the avocado.
Cut into long slices.
Take a small mixing bowl.
Mix the Langoustine tails.
Add the Kewpie mayo, Capelin Roe.
Mix well.
Fill a small bowl with water.
Take the soy paper.
Place it horizontally. 
Place the Langoustine mixture across the centre.
Place the cucumber and avocado.
Dip finger in the small bowl of water.
Wet across the farthest edge away.
Grab the edge of the closest soy paper.
Keep the fillings in place with fingers.
Roll it into a tight cylinder.
Cut the lobster roll into six pieces.
Enjoy with soy sauce and wasabi.
Serve.

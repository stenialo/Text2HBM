Toast the gomashio ingredients.
Toast in a dry frying pan.
Wait for 4 minutes.
Pour the mixture into a blender.
Pulse the mixture for 3 seconds.
Assemble the necessary ingredients for sushi.
Take a medium bowl.
Stir together the rice, gomashio.
Stir the maple syrup and vinegar.
Chill the mixture in a refrigerator.
Wait 15 minutes.
Place the nori-sheet onto the sushi mat.
Take half cup of the rice mixture.
Spread over of the nori-sheet.
Add little vegetable into the centre of rice.
Roll the sushi away.
Use a sharp knife.
Cut into 6 pieces.
Serve.

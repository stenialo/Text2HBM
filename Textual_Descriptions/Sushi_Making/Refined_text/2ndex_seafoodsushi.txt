Cut the seafood into small slices.
Dip your hands in water.
Take a small amount of sushi rice.
Place the wasabi on side of the fish slice.
Place the fish on the rice.
Put the wasabi side on the rice.
Shape both items together.
Hold the tuna and rice with hand.
Press the fish downwards.
Shape it in a rounded rectangle.
Repeat the process using the salmon and shrimp.
Garnish.
Serve.

Combine the cup of sushi rice.
Combine the cup of water.
Put them in the rice cooker.
Stir the ingredients.
Close the lid and hit the start button. 
Wait 15-20 minutes to finish.
Heat vinegar and sugar.
Heat the salt together in the saucepan.
Add the seasoning mixture to the cooked rice.
Let it cool.
Cut the ingredients into small pieces.
Place the nori on rolling mat.
Wet your fingers.
Take a thin layer of sushi rice.
Spread onto the sheet of nori.
Make a shallow depression.
Add the filling ingredients to rice.
Roll the sushi up from the bottom.
Cut roll into pieces.
Add the toppings and condiments.
Serve.

Wash the rice.
Empty the rice into the bowl.
Fill it up with water.
Use your hands to wash the rice.
Cook the rice.
Add the water and rice to saucepan.
Add the salt and sugar to saucepan.
Lower the heat.
Continue to cook the rice.
Wait for 12-15 minutes.
Add the coconut milk.
Allow the rice to cool.
Cut the fruits.
Spread the rice onto plastic wrap.
Place the fruit slices.
Roll the sushi.
Serve.

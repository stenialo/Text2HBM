Rinse the rice in a fine mesh strainer.
Add to a medium saucepan with water.
Boil.
Once it boils reduce heat to low.
Cover and cook.
Wait 15 minutes.
Add the vinegar, sugar to saucepan.
Add the salt to small saucepan.
Heat over medium heat.
Place in a jar.
Cool in the fridge.
Once the rice is done, kill the heat.
Add the cooled vinegar mixture.
Stir.
Prep the veggies.
Chop them into thin pieces.
Grab a thick towel.
Fold it over into a rectangle.
Place it on a flat surface.
Top with the plastic wrap.
Top with a sheet of nori.
Use wet hands.
Pat a thin layer of rice all over nori.
Arrange the serving of your veggies.
Start to roll the nori.
Add the rice over with the fingers.
Once the vegetables are covered.
Roll over the plastic wrap and towel.
Slice with a sharp knife and set aside.
Serve immediately with pickle.

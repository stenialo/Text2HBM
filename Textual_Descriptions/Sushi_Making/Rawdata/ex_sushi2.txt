Ingredients:
	6 sheets sushi seaweed aka nori
	1 batch prepared sushi rice
	1/2 lb sushi-grade raw salmon
	4 oz cream cheese sliced into strips
	1 avocado sliced
	soy sauce
Place the seaweed on a bamboo mat.
Cover the sheet of seaweed with an even layer of prepared sushi rice.
Smooth gently with the rice paddle.
Layer salmon, cream cheese, and avocado on the rice.
Roll it up tightly.
Slice with a sharp knife.
Serve with soya sauce.

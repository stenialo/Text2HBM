Ingredients:
     4 slices whole-wheat bread
     1/2 pound soft tofu
     1/4 cup (60 mL) soy milk or rice milk
     1 teaspoon vanilla extract
     1 teaspoon cinnamon
     1/4 cup (60 mL) water
     1 tablespoon soy margarine
Prepare the egg batter. Combine everything except the butter and bread in a food processor or blender.
Heat the butter in a skillet over medium-high heat.
Soak the bread in the egg batter.
Cook the bread in the skillet until it is browned on both sides.
Serve the dish with fresh fruits or yogurt.

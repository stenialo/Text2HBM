Beat the eggs in a bowl.
In a large bowl crack some eggs.
Whisk the eggs together until well combined.
Put in the spices.
Add the red chilli powder and salt.
Add the turmeric.
Add the ginger garlic paste to the eggs.
Mix well.
Add the milk and mix well.
Put the oil in a Frying pan.
Allow the pan to heat up.
Soak the bread slice in the egg mixture.
Fry.
Cook the bread.
Soak up the excess grease.
Take the bread slices out of the pan.
Place the paper towel on top of the bread.
Apply pressure to soak up.
Serve.

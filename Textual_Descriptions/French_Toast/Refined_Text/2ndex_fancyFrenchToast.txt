Cut thick pieces of bread.
Add the eggs into a bowl.
Add the milk to the eggs.
Add a teaspoon of vanilla.
Add the allspice.
Add the cinnamon.
Add a pinch of salt.
Mix the mixture.
Take the bread.
Soak it in the mixture.
Put it in the refrigerator.
Soak for 30 minutes.
Dip them in mixture again.
Take a nice large skillet.
Melt the spoons of whole butter.
Put the bread on skillet.
Put medium heat.
Make sure that the breads are brown.
Two minutes on each side is best.
Transfer them to your baking sheet.
Heat the oven to 400F.
Cook the bread for 10-12 minutes.
Serve.

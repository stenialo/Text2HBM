Heat the oven to 225C.
Melt the margarine in the baking pan.
Sprinkle the sugar and cinnamon over margarine.
Mix the eggs and salt.
Mix the orange juice and the orange rind.
Soak each side of the bread slice in the mixture.
Place them close together in the prepared pan.
Bake for 15 minutes.
Turn and bake 5 minutes more.
Serve.

Prepare the batter.
Take a large bowl.
Whisk the milk and eggs.
Whisk the flour and vanilla.
Whisk the sugar and salt.
Coat the bread into mixture.
Pour into a casserole dish.
Top the pan off.
Put the nuts, cream cheese and blueberries.
Chill the mixture in refrigerator.
Heat the oven to 400F.
Bake for 20 minutes.
Garnish the dish with sugar.
Ready to serve.

Combine the cornflakes and pecans.
Make the coating.
Set aside.
Divide the banana slices among the bread.
Top with the remaining bread slices.
Make four sandwiches.
Take a large bowl.
Whisk the eggs.
Whisk the milk and vanilla.
Whisk the cinnamon and nutmeg.
Soak the sandwiches in the egg mixture.
Dredge in the the cornflakes mixture.
Press to coat.
Melt the butter in a large skillet.
Put the oven on medium heat.
Add the sandwiches to the skillet.
Cook as crispy.
Serve immediately.

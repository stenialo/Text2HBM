Shutting off the main water supply.
Drain all the remaining water.
Remove a large enough section of the pipe.
Sand the burrs of both ends of the cut pipe.
Remove any debris from inside the pipe.
Install the shut off valve on either side of the system.
Add bypass valve.
Shut all valves.
Install a pressure gauge next to each shut off valve.
Attach a piece of plywood to the back wall.
Make sure the right direction of in and out ports.
Apply teflon tape on the all threaded ends.
Turn the water back on.
Open the valves except for bypass.
Check for leaks.
Flush the entire system.
Follow  the manufacturer instructions.
Close all the outlets.

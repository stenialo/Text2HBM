Steps to Make It

Place boneless, skinless chicken breasts on the bottom of a wide saute pan or skillet with high sides. 
Add enough water to cover the chicken breasts with at least 2 inches of water.
Add some chopped onion,
Add chopped carrot, 
Add chopped celery, 
Add chopped as well as some fresh herbs like thyme or tarragon, 
Add bay leaf 
Add few whole black peppercorns.  
Add a tablespoon of Kosher salt.
Bring the water to a boil.
Then lower it to a simmer.
You should see a few gentle bubbles rise up from time to time, but it should not be at a full rolling boil.
Simmer for about 10 minutes
Then turn off the heat
Cover the pot tightly
Let the chicken breasts stand in the hot water for another 15 to 20 minutes until completely cooked through. 
After 15 to 20 minutes, remove the chicken breasts 
Serve right away 
Pull/shred/chop them into pieces 
Refrigerate.

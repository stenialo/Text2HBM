Steps to Make It


Heat the oven to 325 F.
Make sure to use an oven-safe pan.
Place boneless, skinless chicken. 
Breasts on the bottom of a wide saute pan or skillet with high sides.
Add enough water to cover the chicken breasts with at least 2 inches of water.
Add some chopped onion.
Add chopped carrot. 
Add chopped celery.
Add chopped as well as some fresh herbs like thyme or tarragon.
Add bay leaf.
Add few whole black peppercorns.  
Add a tablespoon of Kosher salt.
Place the pan over medium-high heat.
Bring the water to a boil.
Then lower the heat to a simmer
Cook for 5 minutes.
Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid.
Put it into the oven.
Poach the chicken breasts in the oven for about 15 minutes.
Then remove.
Serve or store.

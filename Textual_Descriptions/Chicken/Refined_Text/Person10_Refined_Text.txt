Steps to Make It

Take a pan.
Then put some oil and butter in the pan.
Then put some butter in the pan.
Chop 3-4 onions.
Put them into the pan.
Then put the chicken.
Add salt. 
Add red chilli. 
Add Cumin. 
Add Turmeric.
After that add pest of Garlic and Ginger mixer.
Then mix them properly.
Marinate them for 30 min.
After that put the pan on the stove.
10 to 15 mins later add some water.
Then cover the pan and wait until it look like gravy and you will see an oil layer around the pan.
Add some salt, if necessary.
Take it off the stove.

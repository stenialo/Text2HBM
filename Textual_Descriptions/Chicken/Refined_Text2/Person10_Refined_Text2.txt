Take a pan.
Add oil to the pan.
Add some butter to the pan.
Chop 3 to 4 onions.
Put the onions into the pan.
Put the chicken into the pan.
Add salt.
Add red chilli.
Add cumin.
Add turmeric.
Add garlic pest.
Add ginger pest.
Mix them all properly.
Marinate them for 30 min.
Put the pan on the stove.
Add water 10 to 15 mins later.
Cover the pan.
Wait until it looks like gravy.
Add salt if necessary.
Off the stove.

Heat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
Take a large bowl.
Add balsamic vinegar.
Add honey.
Add mustard.
Add garlic.
Combine them all.
Season with salt.
Season with pepper.
Whisk until combined.
Add the chicken thighs.
Toss until fully coated.
Put into the fridge to marinate.
Take a medium bowl.
Add potatoes.
Add chopped rosemary.
Season with salt.
Season with black pepper.
Add 1 tablespoon oil.
Toss until combined.
Set aside the bowl.
Take a large ovenproof skillet pan.
Heat tablespoon oil.
Add chicken in the pan.
Sear chicken skin side down 2 minutes.
Flip the chicken.
Sear the chicken again for 2 more minutes.
Add potatoes.
Add rosemary sprigs.
Transfer to the oven.
Bake until the potatoes are tender.
Bake until the chicken is cooked.
Serve the chicken  with pan drippings.
Serve the potatoes with pan drippings.

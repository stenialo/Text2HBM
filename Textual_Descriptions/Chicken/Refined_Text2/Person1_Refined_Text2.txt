Heat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
Remove the neck from the chicken's body.
Remove the giblets from the chicken's body.
Pat the bird dry inside with the paper towels.
Pat the bird dry out with the paper towels.
Smear the outside of the chicken with butter.
Smear the inside of the chicken with butter.
Season chicken with kosher salt inside.
Season chicken with kosher salt out.
Season chicken with freshly ground black pepper inside.
Season chicken with freshly ground black pepper out.
Truss the chicken securely.
Chop about half an onion.
Chop single celery stalk.
Chop single medium carrot.
Scatter these chopped vegetables at the bottom of a roasting pan.
Set roasting rack over the chopped vegetables.
Place the chicken breast side up onto the rack.
Put the roasting pan to the oven.
Chicken has to be roasted for an hour and 15 minutes.
Check the temperature.
The thermometer should read at least 165 degrees Fahrenheit.
Remove the roasting pan from oven.
Lift out the rack with the roasted chicken on it.
Put the chicken on a clean cutting board.
Let it rest there uncovered for 20 minutes.
Place the roasting pan with the roasted vegetables on the stove.
Add a tablespoon of butter.
Heat over medium heat until the butter melts.
Add a tablespoon of flour.
Stir to form a paste.
Now pour about 2 cups of chicken stock into the pan.
Stir to combine.
Bring to a simmer over medium heat until reduced.
Bring to a simmer over medium heat until thickened.
Strain the gravy through a mesh strainer.
Season it to taste with kosher salt and black pepper.

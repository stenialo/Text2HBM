Take a large plastic zipper bag.
Add soy sauce.
Add garlic.
Add vinegar.
Add honey.
Add ginger.
Add onions.
Add sesame oil.
Add sesame seeds.
Combine them all together.
Add the chicken breasts.
Squeeze the excess air out of the bag.
Seal tightly the plastic zipper bag.
Put the bag to the refrigerator.
Remove the bag from the refrigerator before cooking.
Prepare a grill.
Spray a bit of canola cooking spray.
Remove the chicken from the marinade.
Grill 6 minutes per side of the chicken.
Fully cooked.
Garnish with the cilantro leaves.

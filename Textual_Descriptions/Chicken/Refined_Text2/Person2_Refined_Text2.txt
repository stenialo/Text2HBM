Squeeze juice out of one lemon.
Set the juice aside.
Stretch a piece of plastic wrap across work surface.
Place chicken cutlets on plastic.
Lay another piece of plastic wrap on the top.
Use meat mallet.
Flatten the chicken breasts between the layers of plastic wrap.
Take a baking dish.
Add the flour.
Add the salt.
Combine them.
Heat the heavy bottomed saute pan over medium high heat.
Add olive oil.
Heat for 30 seconds.
Add onion to the pan.
Add garlic to the pan.
Saute until onion is slightly translucent.
Remove onion from the pan.
Remove garlic from the pan.
Set them aside.
Add the butter to the pan.
Heat until it turns foamy.
Dredge both sides of the chicken breast.
Cover the chicken in the flour mixture.
Shake off the excess flour.
Add the chicken breast to the hot pan.
Cook 2 to 3 minutes per side.
Remove the chicken from the pan.
Set aside on a plate.
Covered with foil.
Add stock to the pan.
Add sherry to the pan.
Add lemon juice to the pan.
Add capers to the pan.
Return onion garlic mixture to the pan.
Heat it through.
Add a bit more oil.
Return chicken to the pan.
Bring liquid to boil.
Lower liquid to a simmer.
Cook for about 3 minutes.
Add the chopped parsley.
Remove the chicken breasts.
Plate the chicken breasts.
Season with kosher salt and lemon juice.
Serve the chicken.

Place the chicken breasts on the bottom of a wide saute pan.
Add water on the pan.
Add some chopped onion.
Add chopped carrot.
Add chopped celery.
Add chopped fresh herbs.
Add bay leaf.
Add black peppercorns.
Add a tablespoon of kosher salt.
Boil the water.
Simmer for 10 minutes.
Then turn off the heat.
Cover the pot tightly.
Keep the chicken breasts in the hot water until completely cooked through.
Remove chicken breasts after 20 minutes.
Serve right away.

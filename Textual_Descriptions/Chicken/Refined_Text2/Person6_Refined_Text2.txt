Ensure the chicken breasts are an even thickness.
Pound the chicken to half inch.
Season the chicken breasts with salt.
Season with pepper.
Season with other dry seasonings.
Place a skillet on stove.
Heat the olive oil and butter.
Add chicken to the pan.
Cook for 6 minutes.
Flip the chicken.
Cook for an additional 6 minutes.
Check the temperature 165 degrees Fahrenheit.
Remove the chicken breasts from the pan.
Rest them on a plate for 5 minutes.

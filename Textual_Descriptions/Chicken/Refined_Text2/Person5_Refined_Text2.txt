Heat oven to 325 Fahrenheit.
Place the boneless, skinless chicken in the pan.
Add enough water.
Add some chopped onion.
Add chopped carrot.
Add chopped celery.
Add chopped fresh herbs.
Add bay leaf.
Add whole black peppercorns.
Add a tablespoon of kosher salt.
Place the pan over medium high heat.
Bring the water to a boil.
Lower the heat and cook for 5 minutes.
Cover the pan.
Put the pan to the oven.
Poach the chicken breasts in the oven for about 15 minutes.
Remove the pan from the oven.
Serve.

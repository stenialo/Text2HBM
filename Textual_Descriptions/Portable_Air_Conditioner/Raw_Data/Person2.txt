Place the air conditioner near both a sliding glass door and an electrical outlet.
Read the instructions that came with your portable air conditioner carefully.
Save these instructions and any associated warranty information for future use.
Ensure that the air conditioner will not be a tripping hazard, and that the airflow will not be obstructed by furniture, plants, etc.
Determine if the window adapter kit will work with your window. 
For the unit to function well, the gaps between the sliding glass door adapter for the vent hose and the sides of the door must be sealed off.
Measure your sliding door opening to determine if the door adapter kit should be expanded or trimmed to fit correctly.
Connect the door connection bracket or adapter to the other end of the exhaust hose, if it is not already attached.
Run the exhaust hose to the glass door, and place the door connection bracket or adapter into the open door.
Secure the exhaust hose door connection in place.
Plug in your air conditioner.

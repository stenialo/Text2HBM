Slide the window open. 
Place the sealing plate into the window gap and adjust the length so it fits the window.
Bolt or screw the sealing plate so its length stays fixed. Close the window.
Attach the exhaust pipe to the air conditioner.
Attach the flat exhaust pipe nozzle to the other end of the exhaust pipe.
Extend the exhaust pipe and insert the exhaust pipe nozzle into the window sealing plate.
Plug the unit into the power point.
Switch it on.
Adjust the temperature.

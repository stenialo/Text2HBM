Pick a mug.
Add two tablespoons of brown sugar.
Add half teaspoon of vanilla extract.
Add 3 tablespoons of flour.
Crack an egg into the mug.
Add butter.
Add a tablespoon of chocolate chips.
Mix all of them together.
Put the mug in microwave for 50 seconds.
50 seconds later check by sticking a toothpick.
Toothpick should come out clean.

Spray a medium microwave safe bowl.
Set aside.
Take a mixing bowl.
Add butter in the mixing bowl.
Add sugar in the mixing bowl.
Add egg in the mixing bowl.
Add vanilla extract in the mixing bowl.
Add milk in the mixing bowl.
Combine them.
Blend together.
Add flour.
Add cocoa powder.
Add baking powder.
Add salt.
Combine again.
Blend into the mixture until smooth.
Pour into the greased bowl.
Cover the bowl.
Cook in the microwave for 2 to 2 minutes 30 seconds.
Cool the cake for 5 minutes.
Cover the bowl with a plate.
Turn the bowl upside down.
Turn the plate upside down.

Take a large bowl.
Add flour.
Add salt.
Add baking powder.
Add baking soda.
Mix them together properly.
Take another bowl.
Beat eggs.
Add powdered sugar.
Take a mixer bowl.
Add butter in the bowl.
Pour the beaten eggs in the batter.
Pour vanilla essence in the batter.
Stir well.
Add the maida.
Mix well.
Pour milk in the mixer.
Mix all ingredients gentle.
Take a baking tray.
Grease with butter.
Pour mixture into baking tray.
Place tray in a microwave oven.
Bake for 30 minutes.
Cake is ready for serve.

Take a medium bowl.
Add flour.
Add cocoa powder.
Add baking powder.
Whisk together.
Add milk in the mix.
Add vegetable oil in the mix.
Whisk until the batter is smooth.
Pour batter into a microwave safe mug.
Add hazelnut chocolate spread in the middle of batter.
Place a paper towel into microwave.
Set the mug on top.
Microwave mug cake for 70 seconds on high.
Carefully remove the mug from microwave.

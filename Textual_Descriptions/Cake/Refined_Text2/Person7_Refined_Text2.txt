Take a large bowl.
Add self rising flour.
Add cocoa powder.
Add baking powder.
Add salt.
Combine all together.
Sift through a strainer.
Add sugar.
Add milk.
Add oil.
Whisk for 2 to 4 minutes for well blended.
Grease a round microwavable bowl.
Make sure the bowl is half filled.
Pick up the bowl.
Tap it on the counter.
Release the inside bubbles.
Place the bowl the microwave.
Set the timer to 7 minutes on high power.
Use the skewer to check for the doneness.
Place a plate underneath the cake bowl.
Flip the plate over.

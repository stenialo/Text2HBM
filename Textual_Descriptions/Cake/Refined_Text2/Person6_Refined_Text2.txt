Take a mixing bowl.
Add butter.
Add sugar.
Add eggs.
Add milk.
Mix all of them thoroughly.
Beat well for 5 mins.
Add flour.
Add baking powder.
Mix all of them properly.
Blend all together.
Pour mixture into a large microwave bowl.
Bake the bowl for 60 seconds in a 600 watt microwave.
Cool the baked bowl.
Cut the cake into wedges.
Serve the cake.

Take a medium bowl.
Add melted butter.
Add sugar.
Whisk until well combined.
Add baking powder.
Add flour.
Add eggs.
Add milk.
Whisk.
Spray the ramekins.
Fill the ramekins a little over halfway.
Tap the ramekins on the counter.
Knock the inside air.
Cover them with a piece of wrap.
Cook in the microwave for 50 to 60 seconds.
Take off the plastic wrap after cooked.
Serve the cake.

Ingredients

1/2 cup - melted Butter.
1/2 cup - Sugar.
1 cup - Flour/Maida.
1 tsp - Baking powder.
2 - Egg.
4 tbsp - Milk.
1 tsp - Vanilla essence.

Steps to Make It

Add sieve flour. 
Add baking powder. 
Mix together.
Keep them aside.
Add butter.
Add sugar.
Add eggs.
Add milk.
Mix thoroughly.
Beat well for 5 mins.
Add flour.
Add baking powder. 
Mix.
Mix and add essence.
Blend again.
Pour the mixture into a large microwave safe bowl.
Bake for 60 seconds in a 600W microwave.
Cool.
Cut into wedges.
Serve.

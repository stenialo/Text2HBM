Ingredients

1 egg.
2 tablespoons brown sugar.
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract.
3 tablespoons self-rising flour.
1 tablespoon butter or margarine.
2 tablespoons chocolate chips.

Steps to Make It

Pick your mug. 
Add two tablespoons of brown sugar or honey to the mug. 
Add 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract to the mug. 
Add 3 tablespoons (44.4 ml) of flour to the mug.
Crack an egg into the mug.
Add butter.
Add a tablespoon or two of chocolate chips (optional).
For mix it all together use a spoon to stir up the contents of the mug.
Put the mug in the microwave for 50 seconds on full power. 
After 50 seconds, check to see if the cake is baked by sticking a toothpick into it. 
The toothpick should come out clean. 
If there is gooey cake residue on the toothpick, put the mug back in the microwave and cook it in 30 second increments until done.

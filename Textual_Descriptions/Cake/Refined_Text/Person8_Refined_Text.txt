Ingredients

1 cup all purpose flour.
1/3 teaspoon baking powder.
2 cup egg.
1/2 teaspoon baking soda.
20 gm Raisins.
3/4 cup powdered sugar.
1/4 cup milk.
3/4 cup butter.
1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence.
20 cashews.
1 pinch salt.


Steps to Make It

Take a large bowl.
Add all purpose flour (maida) in a bowl.
Add salt in a bowl.
Add baking powder in a bowl.
Add baking soda in a bowl in a bowl.
Take another bowl.
Beat eggs. 
Add powdered sugar.
Pour the beaten eggs in the batter.
Pour vanilla essence in the batter.
Stir well. 
Ensure no lumps are formed.
Add the maida.
Mix well.
Pour milk.
Mix until all ingredients are combined well.
Take a baking tray.
Grease with butter.
Pour the mixture into it.
Place the tray in a microwave oven.
Bake for 30 minutes or until the cake rises to the top and turns slightly brownish. 
To ensure that the cake is baked well by inserting a knife or toothpick, if it comes out clean, cake is ready.

Ingredients

1/2 cup - melted Butter.
1/2 cup - Sugar.
1 cup - Flour/Maida.
1 tsp - Baking powder.
2 - Egg.
4 tbsp - Milk.
1 tsp - Vanilla essence.

Steps to Make It

Mix sieve flour and baking powder together and keep them aside.
Mix butter, sugar, eggs and milk thoroughly.
Beat well for 5 mins.
Add flour and baking powder mix.
Mix and add essence.
Blend again and pour the mixture into a large microwave safe bowl.
Bake for 60 seconds in a 600W microwave.
Cool, cut into wedges and serve.

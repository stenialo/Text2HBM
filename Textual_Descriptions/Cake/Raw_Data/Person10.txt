Ingredients

2 tablespoons Butter (melted).
2 1/2 tablespoons Sugar.
2 tablespoons Milk.
1/2 teaspoon Vanilla extract.
1/2 teaspoon Baking Powder.
1/4 cup All Purpose Flour (or Maida + 1 tablespoons).
7 or 8 Blueberries.


Steps to Make It

Take a bowl, whisk together butter, sugar, milk and vanilla extract.
Add baking powder and flour and whisk them till combined.
Mix in blueberries and pour into one mug.
Make sure that mugs or bowls are only half-filled.
Microwave for max 1 minute on high.
Serve the blueberry mug cake dusted with sugar.

Heat the oven to 350F.
Lightly spoon the flour into cup.
Level with a knife.
Add the flour.
Add the cocoa.
Add the baking powder.
Add the baking soda.
Add the salt.
Stir with a whisk.
Place the sugar in the bowl.
Place the butter in the bowl.
Beat with a mixer.
Add the vanilla and the eggs.
Beat well.
With the mixer add flour mixture.
Beat well.
Fold the  cherries and chocolate chips.
Drop by tablespoonfuls onto baking sheets.
Bake at 350F.
Wait 12 minutes.
Remove from the oven.
Cool on the pans.
Wait for 5 minutes.
Cool completely on the wire racks.
Serve.

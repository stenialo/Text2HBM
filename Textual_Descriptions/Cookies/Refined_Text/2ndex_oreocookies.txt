Heat the oven to 170C.
Combine the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Add the flour to the bowl.
Add the cocoa powder to the bowl.
Add the baking soda to the bowl.
Add the salt to the bowl.
Add the baking powder to the bowl.
Mix the butter in the bowl.
Mix the sugar in the bowl.
Add the eggs. 
Mix the eggs with the butter and sugar.
Scoop the dry ingredients with the mixer.
Line the cookie pan with the parchment paper.
Scoop the dough onto paper.
Flatten the cookie dough balls.
Bake the cookies for 15 minutes.

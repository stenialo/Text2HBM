Heat the oven to 350F.
Beat the butter and the sugar.
Place the butter in bowl.
Place the white and brown sugar in bowl.
Add the eggs and vanilla.
Mix the dry ingredients.
Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
Stir in the raisins.
Scoop out the cookies.
Bake the cookies.
Place them in the preheated oven.
Bake for 10 minutes.

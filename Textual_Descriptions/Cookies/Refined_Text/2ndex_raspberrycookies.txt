Heat the oven to 375F.
Mix the butter and sugar together.
Add the eggs and the vanilla.
Combine the flour in the bowl.
Combine the salt in the bowl.
Combine the baking soda in the bowl.
Gradually add the dry ingredients to wet ingredients.
Stir the chocolate chips.
Add gently raspberries.
Put the cookies on a cookie sheet.
Bake the cookies.
Let the cookies cool.

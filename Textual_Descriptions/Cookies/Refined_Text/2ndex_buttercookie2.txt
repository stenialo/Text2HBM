Take a large bowl.
Cream the butter.
Cream the white sugar.
Beat in the egg.
Stir in the vanilla. 
Combine the flour and salt.
Stir them into the sugar.
Cover the dough.
Chill the dough for 1 hour.
Chill the cookie sheets.
Heat the oven to 400F.
Press the dough on sheets.
Bake for 8 minutes in the oven.
Remove from the sheets to cool.

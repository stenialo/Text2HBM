Ingredients:
   3/4 cup (170 g) butter, softened
   1/2 cup (100 g) sugar
   1/2 teaspoon of salt
   1 large egg
   2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
   2 cups (240 g) all-purpose flour
   1 cup (125 g) chopped pecans or walnuts
   1 cup (6 ounces or 175 g) of mini-chocolate chips
   Powdered sugar for rolling the cookies
Heat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 C).
Cream the butter, sugar, and salt.
Stir in the egg and vanilla extract.
Add the flour, pecans, and mini-chocolate chips.
Shape the cookies.
Bake the snowball cookies.
Roll the cookies in powdered sugar.
Finished.

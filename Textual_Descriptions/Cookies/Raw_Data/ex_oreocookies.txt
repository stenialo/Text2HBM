Ingredients:
   3 cups flour
   3/4 cup cocoa powder
   1 teaspoon baking soda
   1/4 teaspoon salt
   1/4 teaspoon baking powder
   3 sticks of butter (softened)
   2 cups sugar
   2 eggs
   extra granulated sugar for topping the cookie dough
Heat oven to 170-180 degree C.
Combine the dry ingredients (except sugar) in a large bowl. Add the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, salt, and baking powder to one of your large mixing bowls.
Mix the butter and sugar in a large bowl.
Add the eggs. After the butter and sugar are well combined, add in the eggs and mix the eggs with the butter and sugar.
Scoop in dry ingredients with the mixer running.
Line a cookie pan with parchment paper.
Scoop the dough onto the parchment paper.
Flatten the cookie dough balls.
Bake the cookies for 15 to 17 minutes.

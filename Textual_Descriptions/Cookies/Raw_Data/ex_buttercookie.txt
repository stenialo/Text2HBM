Ingredients:
   1 cup (225 grams) butter
   1 cup (200 grams) white sugar
   1 egg
   2 teaspoons (10 mL) vanilla essence
   ¼ teaspoon (1.42 grams) salt
   2 ⅔ cups (241 grams) all-purpose flour
Preheat your oven to 200 °C.
Measure 1 cup of butter and 1 cup of sugar into a mixing bowl.
Cream the butter and sugar until they are light and fluffy.
Stir the egg and vanilla into the mixture.
Mix the salt and flour in a separate bowl.
Add the dry ingredients to the batter.
Cover the bowl of dough and place it in the fridge for 1 hour.
Use a cookie press if you want to get pretty shapes.
Roll the dough into a log if you want to make circle cookies.
Cut the log into 1 cm (0.39 in) slices and place them onto a baking tray.
Bake the cookies for 10 minutes or until they are golden brown.
Finished.

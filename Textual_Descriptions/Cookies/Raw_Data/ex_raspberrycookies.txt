Ingredients:
    1⁄2 cup (120 ml) (1 stick) of softened butter
    1⁄2 cup (120 ml) brown sugar
    1⁄2 cup (120 ml) white sugar
    2 eggs
    1 teaspoon (5 ml) vanilla extract
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1 teaspoon baking soda
    2 1/2 cups of flour
    1/2 to 1 cup (235 ml) of fresh raspberries
    1 cup chocolate chips
Heat your oven to 375 °F (191 °C).
Mix the butter and sugars together.
Add the eggs and vanilla.
Combine the flour, salt, and baking soda in a separate bowl.
Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ones.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
Fold in the raspberries very gently.
Put the cookies on a non-stick cookie sheet or onto a greased cookie sheet.
Bake the cookies.
Let the cookies cool.

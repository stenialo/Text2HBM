Ingredients:
	1 cup butter
	1 cup white sugar
	1 egg
	2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
	1/4 teaspoon salt
	2 teaspoons vanilla extract
In a large bowl, cream together the butter and white sugar until they are light and fluffy.
Beat in an egg, then stir in the vanilla. 
Mix the flour and salt, then stir them into the butter and sugar.
Cover dough, and chill for at least one hour. Chill cookie sheets.
Heat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
Press dough out onto ungreased, chilled cookie sheets.
Bake in the oven for 8 to 10 minutes or until they become golden at the edges.
Remove from cookie sheets to cool on wire racks.

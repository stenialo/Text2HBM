Ingredients:
	1 tablespoon white granulated sugar
	1 tablespoon brown sugar
	1 1/2 tablespoon butter
	1-1/2 tablespoons chocolate chips
	1 tablespoon water
	1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
	Pinch of salt
	Pinch of baking soda
	4 tablespoons flour

Heat the oven to 350°F or 180°C.
Put all the ingredients in a small bowl. Mix and mash thoroughly with a fork.
Put the dough in the center of a greased cookie sheet.
Bake for 10-12 minutes.
Let it cool on a plate and enjoy.
